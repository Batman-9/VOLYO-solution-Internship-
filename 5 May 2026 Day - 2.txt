**Day 2: DBMS Architecture & Data Models**

**Topic:**
DBMS Architecture and Data Models

**Learning Objectives:**

* Understand the 3-schema architecture (External, Conceptual, Internal levels)
* Learn how data is organized and managed at different levels
* Explore different data models: hierarchical, network, and relational
* Understand the differences between these models with simple examples

**Activities / Deliverables:**

* Draw a 3-tier (3-schema) architecture diagram
* Compare hierarchical, network, and relational models
* Write short examples for each data model
* Prepare notes explaining how each model works

**Tools / Tech:**

* Draw.io (for diagrams)
* MS Word (for notes and comparison)
